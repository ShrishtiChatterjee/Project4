About Typeface

'Beast is a decorative variable typeface that is inspired by small beasts, namely the spotted lanternfly, as observed in the year 2025 and the seeds of the maple or samara. This is ideal in its use as a display typeface or as a drop cap to mark the beginning of a chapter.
